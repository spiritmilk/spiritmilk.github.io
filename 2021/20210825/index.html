变量替换巧获积分关系

题源：自动内卷人（来自Q群-卷明白）

证明: ∫01ln⁡(1+x)xdx=π212\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (1+x)}{x} d x=\frac{\pi^{2}}{12}∫01​xln(1+x)​dx=12π2​, 并由此计算 ∫0+∞arctan⁡x⋅ln⁡(1+1x2)dx\int_{0}^{+\infty} \arctan x \cdot \ln \left(1+\frac{1}{x^{2}}\right) d x∫0+∞​arctanx⋅ln(1+x21​)dx.

前半部分：略

后半部分：

计算∫0+∞arctan⁡x⋅ln⁡(1+1x2)dx\int_{0}^{+\infty} \arctan x \cdot \ln \left(1+\frac{1}{x^{2}}\right) d x∫0+∞​arctanx⋅ln(1+x21​)dx并不算很困难，但由前半部分证明后半部分存在一定难度。

相似的题：

SEEMOUS 2016 problem 4:

整数n≥1n \ge 1n≥1。设$$I_n=\int_0^{\infty}\frac{\arctan x}{(1+x^2)^n} dx.$$
证明
a) ∑n=1∞Inn=π26\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{I_n}{n}=\frac{\pi ^2}{6}∑n=1∞​nIn​​=6π2​
b)∫0∞arctan⁡⋅ln⁡(1+1x2)dx=π26\int_0^{\infty}\arctan\cdot \ln(1+\frac{1}{x^2})dx=\frac{\pi ^2}{6}∫0∞​arctan⋅ln(1+x21​)dx=6π2​

∫0∞arctan⁡(x)ln⁡(1+1x2)dx\int_0^\infty \arctan(x) \ln \left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right) dx∫0∞​arctan(x)ln(1+x21​)dx

=∫0∞ln⁡(1+1x2)d(xarctanx−12ln(1+x2))=\int_0^\infty \ln \left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right) d(xarctanx-\frac{1}{2}ln(1+x^2))=∫0∞​ln(1+x21​)d(xarctanx−21​ln(1+x2))

$= \int_0^\infty \left[\frac{2 \arctan{x}}{1+x^2} - \frac{\ln(1+x^2)}{x (1+x^2)}\right] dx $

$\hspace{-11pt}\stackrel{x = \sqrt{\mathrm{e}^t - 1}}{=} \frac{\pi^2}{4} - \frac{1}{2} \int_0^\infty \frac{t}{\mathrm{e}^t - 1} \mathrm{d} t $

设:

I=∫0∞xex−1dxI=\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x}{e^{x}-1} d xI=∫0∞​ex−1x​dx

则：

I=∫0∞xex−1dx=−∫−∞0−ye−y−1dy=∫−∞0y⋅dyy−1dy=∫01u⋅ln⁡(u)u⋅(u−1)duI=\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x}{e^{x}-1} d x=-\int_{-\infty}^{0} \frac{-y}{e^{-y}-1} d y=\int_{-\infty}^{0} \frac{y \cdot d^{y}}{y-1} d y=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{u \cdot \ln (u)}{u \cdot(u-1)} d uI=∫0∞​ex−1x​dx=−∫−∞0​e−y−1−y​dy=∫−∞0​y−1y⋅dy​dy=∫01​u⋅(u−1)u⋅ln(u)​du
I=∫01ln⁡(u)u−1du=4∫01v⋅ln⁡(v)v2−1dν=∫01u⋅ln⁡(u)u2−1du+∫01ln⁡(u)u2−1duI=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (u)}{u-1} d u=4 \int_{0}^{1} \frac{v \cdot \ln (v)}{v^{2}-1} d \nu=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{u \cdot \ln (u)}{u^{2}-1} d u+\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (u)}{u^{2}-1} d uI=∫01​u−1ln(u)​du=4∫01​v2−1v⋅ln(v)​dν=∫01​u2−1u⋅ln(u)​du+∫01​u2−1ln(u)​du (1)

由此说明：

∫01ln⁡(x)x2−1dx=3∫01xln⁡(x)x2−1dx\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (x)}{x^{2}-1} d x=3\int_{0}^{1} \frac{x\ln (x)}{x^{2}-1} d x∫01​x2−1ln(x)​dx=3∫01​x2−1xln(x)​dx （2）

除此之外，还有一个有趣的等式：

$ \int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (u)}{u^{2}-1} d u =- \cdot \int_{\infty}^{1} \frac{\left(\frac{1}{w}\right)^{2} \cdot \ln \left(\frac{1}{w}\right)}{\left(\frac{1}{w}\right)^{2}-1} d w = \int_{1}^{\infty} \frac{\ln (w)}{w^{2}-1} d v = \int_{1}^{\infty} \frac{\ln (u)}{u^{2}-1} d u$

设J=∫01ln⁡(x+1)xdxJ=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (x+1)}{x} d xJ=∫01​xln(x+1)​dx，由题意我们知道J=π212J=\frac{\pi^{2}}{12}J=12π2​.

J=∫01ln⁡(x+1)xdxJ=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (x+1)}{x} d xJ=∫01​xln(x+1)​dx
=分部积分−∫01ln⁡(x)x+1dx\hspace{-11pt}\stackrel{分部积分}{=} -\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (x)}{x+1} d x=分部积分−∫01​x+1ln(x)​dx
=−∫01(x−1)⋅ln⁡(x)(x−1)⋅(x+1)dx=-\int_{0}^{1} \frac{(x-1) \cdot \ln (x)}{(x-1) \cdot(x+1)} d x=−∫01​(x−1)⋅(x+1)(x−1)⋅ln(x)​dx
=∫01ln⁡(x)x2−1dx−∫01x⋅ln⁡(x)x2−1dx=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (x)}{x^{2}-1} d x-\int_{0}^{1} \frac{x \cdot \ln (x)}{x^{2}-1} d x=∫01​x2−1ln(x)​dx−∫01​x2−1x⋅ln(x)​dx

代入（2）得：
∫01ln⁡(x)x2−1dx=π28∫01xln⁡(x)x2−1dx=π224\quad \int_{0}^{1} \frac{\ln (x)}{x^{2}-1} d x=\frac{\pi^{2}}{8} \quad \int_{0}^{1} \frac{x \ln (x)}{x^{2}-1} d x=\frac{\pi^{2}}{24}∫01​x2−1ln(x)​dx=8π2​∫01​x2−1xln(x)​dx=24π2​
再代入（1）得:I=π26I=\frac{\pi^{2}}{6}I=6π2​

故原式=π24−12∗I=π26=\frac{\pi^{2}}{4}-\frac{1}{2}*I=\frac{\pi^{2}}{6}=4π2​−21​∗I=6π2​
